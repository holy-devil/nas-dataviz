What makes an acheiver?

The National Achievement Survey of 2014 conducted by NCERT provides an insight to the learning achievement of children undergoing elementary education.
We have analysed the dataset to understand how the various factors impact the learning and performance of VIIIth standard students thus surveyed.

6722 Schools
1,84,670 Class VIII students
700 districts in 36 States/UTs
Out of total of 1,84,670 students,
1,18,575 students (64.2%) failed in at least one subject underachievers
6,306 students (3.4%) could secure grade A and above without failing in the rest of the subjects achievers.

Breaking it down subject-wise, Only a fraction of students seem to be achievers - the basic minimum of securing grade A/A+ in any one subject while not failing in the rest.

What makes these fraction of achievers different from the rest?
